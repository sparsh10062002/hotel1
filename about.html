About Our Hotel

Wood Castle Spa & Resort is a luxurious and Eco-friendly jungle resort spread over 8 acres of lush green grassland with large number of mango trees, rich flora, exotic mixed shrubs in Corbett. The resort offers best ambiance, facilities and services. Above all professionally trained and responsible staff with personalized service manages it.

Wood Castle is a suitable destination for Corporate Conferences, Leisure trips, Incentive programs, Workshops for Team building Programs, Adventure tours etc.
